About

This organisation organises tree plantation activities and inculcates the sense of responsibility among the young students across Rajasthan. The name of this program is ‘Go Green Kids.’ They provide free saplings in schools & colleges and let the students plant and take care of those saplings. Also, they regularly check up to see how the trees are and surprisingly have a 95 percent survival rate so far. Taking it ahead, they are now planning to expand their operations to schools and colleges across India.
